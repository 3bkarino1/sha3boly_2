Historical Space Weather Events

1859
Carrington Event - The most powerful solar storm on record, causing telegraph systems to fail worldwide.
1989
Quebec Blackout - A geomagnetic storm caused a 9-hour power outage affecting 6 million people.
2003
Halloween Storms - Series of solar flares caused satellite malfunctions and airline communication issues.
2012
Near Miss - A Carrington-level solar storm narrowly missed Earth, which could have caused trillions in damages.
2022
SpaceX Satellite Loss - A geomagnetic storm destroyed 40 Starlink satellites shortly after launch.
